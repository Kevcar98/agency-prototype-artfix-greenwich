ABOUT
ARTFIX

artfix builds communities through the power of creativity. Our creative spaces operate as one-stop platforms, empowering you to share your creative ideas with the world.

"From conception to execution — artfix is the combination of a café, school, workspace, and venue that emulates the creative process in four steps: meet, learn, work, show."

THE CREATIVE PROCESS

MEET

Connect with local creatives in our vibrant cafe space.

LEARN

Acquire new skills through community-led workshops.

WORK

Develop your projects in our dedicated co-working areas.

SHOW

Exhibit and perform in our versatile venue spaces.

LIFE AS ART —
empowering everyday creators.
